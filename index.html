About me

Hello there!
My name is Saurabh Dhuri. I am an MCA (Master of Computer Applications) Graduate from SIES College of Management Studies, Nerul, India.

Currently, I am a UI Designer at DoWell Research Lab, who is on a steep learning curve and is looking for some better opportunities out there in UX.
My greatest desire is to pursue my career in UX and work, thrive, and survive in this industry. I am extremely worried that if I pursue a career outside of UX, then I may not find myself working in the field that I am most passionate about.

I look forward to working on some great projects that are not only pleasing to the eyes but also solve some real-world problems. I don't want to be known as the new Designer/Developer on the block, but I want to be known as the Problem Solver.
